# 🚀 Vendor Onboarding Quick-Start

Use this guide tomorrow morning to get your first vendors onto the platform.

## Option 1: The Professional Invite (Recommended)
1. Log in to your [Supabase Dashboard](https://supabase.com/dashboard).
2. Navigate to **Authentication** -> **Users**.
3. Click **Invite User**.
4. Enter the vendor's email address.
5. They will receive a branded email to "Activate Their Account."

## Option 2: The "VIP Backdoor" (Use if emails are slow)
Send this link directly to the vendor via text or email:
**`https://submittal-tracker-gilt.vercel.app?signup=true`**

*Instruction to Vendor:* "Use this link to access the private portal. Click 'Sign Up' at the bottom to create your account instantly."

---

## The First Login (What to tell them)
Once they have set their password and logged in:
1. They will see a clean, empty dashboard.
2. Tell them: **"Click '+ New Project' to start your first submittal tracking island."**
3. Once they create a project, they can use the **Spec Intel Hub** to upload their first PDF.
