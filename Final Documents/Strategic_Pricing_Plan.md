# 💼 Submittal Tracker Pro: Business & Beta Playbook

## 1. 💰 Tiered Pricing Model (The Pivot)
Construction firms have varying needs. Offer these three tiers to capture everyone:

| Tier | Projects | Price | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Solo Job** | 1 Active Project | **$149 / Mo** | Single-project vendors |
| **The Project Team** | Up to 10 Projects | **$399 / Mo** | Mid-size subcontractors |
| **The Enterprise** | Unlimited | **$899 / Mo** | Large GC firms |

---

## 🏗️ 2. The Insurance Value Prop
You aren't selling a "tool"; you are selling **Risk Management.**
*   **Submittals are the "Liquidated Damages" trap.** If an item is missed, the building doesn't open.
*   Your app provides the **Verified Paper Trail** that protects the company from lawsuits and project delays.

---

## 🧪 3. The 14-Day Beta Roadmap (Tomorrow's Launch)
Don't charge money yet. Collect **Feedback and Wins.**

### Day 1-3: Onboarding
*   Invite your first vendor (and yourself).
*   **Goal:** Watch them use the **Spec Intel Hub** to split out a large PDF.
*   **Victory Check:** Did they find it easier than manual entry?

### Day 4-10: Active Tracking
*   Ask them to upload a few submittals and a revision.
*   **Goal:** Have them use the **Activity Log** to see the history.
*   **Victory Check:** Do they feel more "in control" of the job?

### Day 11-14: The "Close"
*   Meet with them. Ask: *"How would you feel if I took the site away tonight and you had to go back to Excel?"*
*   **If they hate the idea of going back to Excel, THEY ARE SOLD.**
*   **Action:** Transition them to a paid tier.

---

## 💾 4. Managing Storage (The Owner's Duty)
*   **Base Tier:** 100GB (Enough for ~50,000 files).
*   **Auto-Scalability:** Supabase handles overflows automatically via small overage fees.
*   **Monitoring:** Check the "Storage" tab in Supabase to see your total "Gigabytes Used."
